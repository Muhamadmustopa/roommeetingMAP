General Affairs (GA)

Maintaining the efficiency, comfortable, and productivity work environment of the entire Mitra Asa Pratama.

About Departemen GA

The General Affairs Department is responsible for ensuring that all non-productive operational activities run smoothly, including the management of office facilities, assets, security, cleanliness, and department relations.

We are committed to creating a comfortable, efficient and productive work environment for all employees at PT Mitra Asa Pratama..
